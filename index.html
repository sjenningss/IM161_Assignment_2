Exploring Around The World

Traditional clothing is when people expresses an identity through a collection of garments, jewelery, acessories rooted in cultural roots of a identifiable group of people. It can generally be associated with a geographic area, or a period of time in history. some clothes can be an indication of social, martial or religious status.

Kahazhstan-Chapans

Kazakhstan which is a Central Asian country and former Soviet republic, extends from the Caspian Sea in the west to the Altai Mountains at its eastern border with China and Russia. A staple article of Kazakh clothing is the chapan or shapan, a long, loose robe. Unlike other clothing, it is not gender-specific and is worn by men and women. Chapans are made from various fabrics and are available in a range of colors (most often monochrome or dark). They are lined with a layer of wool or cotton.
