Germany-Tracht-Dirdndls-Lederhosen

Tracht is a way of dressing with a particular group of people which is worn by men.Lederhosen is short or knee-length leather breeches which is traditional garments. Dirdndls are a feminine dress worn by women and was issued by Nazis as pan-German idenity.

France-Coiffe

In Brittany france women wear a coiffe, which a lace headress and seen in local festivials or pardons. Some coiffes can be tall as 30-40cm tall.
