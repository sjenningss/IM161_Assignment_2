Asia

Let's look at some traditional clothing!

Japan-Kimono

Kimonos are worn by both men, women and children. These were mainly worn during the 17th century and were even used as self expression. They are still worn today for festivials, events, and even marriage.

Vietnam-Ao Dai

The Ao Dai is a symbol of beauty and can be worn by people of all ages.These can be worn at special photoshoots, ocassions, school events, The traditional clothing was made from several pieces of cloth that is sewn together in flaps to make make the front and back of the lower part of the long garment, which appeared to make the garment split down the sides, a feature that has been preserved in the modern version of the Ao Dai.
